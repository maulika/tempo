Welcome To The Complete African Recipe

The completeAfricanrecipe is an on-going effort to bring together the over 205,000 traditional recipes that is native to the Africans, African diaspora and the Caribbean.

We have put together this collection to enhance the act of cooking African dishes. A considerable amount of work has gone into building this resource to provide a sort of an encyclopedia on African dishes. The writing style has been simplified and made for easy understanding and greater comprehension for both a professional and an amateur cook.

For those who love to eat good food, thecompleteafricanrecipe aims to provide that elusive link between a 5 star professional chef and the comfort of your kitchen. This is an on-going work; we strive to capture every existing recipe out there that is indigenous to Africa.

Please do not hesitate to send us your recipe on African dishes.

Thank you.
